Getting started
Step 1: Download the Unminable App

    Go to the official Unminable website (unmineable.com) and find the "Get Started" or "Download" section.

    Download the mining app suitable for your PC (Windows version recommended for beginners).

    Once downloaded, unzip the file if needed and find the installer or executable.

Step 2: Launch and Setup the App

    Open the Unminable app by double-clicking the executable file.

    The app will load and detect your hardware (CPU and GPU).

    Click "Continue" to proceed after hardware detection.

Image suggestion: Screenshot of the app's opening screen showing hardware detected and the "Continue" button.
Step 3: Choose Your Mining Coin

    You'll see a list of supported coins and tokens that you can mine.

    Pick your favorite coin to mine by clicking on it.

    The app will automatically select the best algorithm for mining that coin.

Step 4: Enter Your Wallet Address

    Enter the wallet address for the coin you chose. This is where your mined rewards will be sent.

    Double-check the wallet address for correctness.

    After entering the address, click "Next" to continue.

Step 5: Configure Mining Settings

    You can choose to mine with CPU, GPU, or both if your hardware supports it.

    Generally, GPU mining is faster and more efficient; select accordingly.

    Adjust any intensity or performance settings if you like, or leave default for ease.

Step 6: Start Mining

    Click the "Start" button to begin mining.

    The app will connect to the Unminable pool and start hashing.

    Monitor your hash rate and mining progress on the app screen.

Step 7: Monitor and Manage

    You can leave the app running while doing other tasks.

    Check periodically to see your mining stats and payouts.

    When you reach the payout threshold, earnings will be transferred to your wallet.

Tips for Best Experience

    Keep your PC plugged in and cool to avoid overheating.

    Use a reliable internet connection for stable mining.

    Consider mining coins with lower difficulty for faster initial payouts.

This tutorial presents simple, friendly instructions with clear visuals to make the Unminable app easy for anyone to start mining cryptocurrency, even beginners.
